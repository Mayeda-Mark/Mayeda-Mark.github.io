About the App

How to use this app

  • Enter the principle amount of your loan and select the length of your loan.
  • Either enter the down payment amount or the percentage of the principle that you wish to pay as a down payment.
  • Either select the state that you wish to purchase a home in to enter that state's average mortgage loan interest rate or manually enter an interest rate into the interest box.
  • Click or tap on the "Calculate Monthly Payment" button to display the monthly payment of your mortgage loan.
  • Click or tap on the "Display Payment Table" to see a breakdown of your monthly payments and how much money is being used to pay off your interest and how much money is being used to pay off your principle amount.
  • Enter all numbers without dollar signs or commas.
