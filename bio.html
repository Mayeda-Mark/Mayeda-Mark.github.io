Bio

Hey. I'm Mark Mayeda. I'm a 35 year old husband and father of 2 who lives in Auburn, WA. I was bron in Boise, ID but grew up in Salem, OR and have lived in New York, NY and Honolulu and Hilo, HI.

In my free time, I like to play video games, read good books, play guitar and watch movies and television. I also enjoy camping, hiking, snowboarding, swimming and surfing. My favorite game of all time is Skyrim, my favorite band is the Beatles, my favorite author is Jim Butcher, my favorite movie is Avengers: Endgame and my favorite TV show is Rick and Morty unless you can isolate the first 11 seasons of the Simpsons.

I have worked at the Federal Aviation Administration since 2014. I began as an air traffic controller but moved in to a position as a flight data specialist in 2017. In December 2019, I completed my BS degree majoring in software engineering from Brigham Young University-Idaho. I am a full stack developer and know and understand the Java, C#, C++, JavaScript and Node.js programming languages very well. I am also experienced in HTML/CSS, AWS, Swagger, PHP and pSQL.
